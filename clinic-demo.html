FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I see results?
Most patients notice changes within the first 2–3 weeks. Significant, sustainable results are typically seen within 4–8 weeks depending on your program and consistency.
Do I need to stop eating my favourite foods?
Absolutely not! We believe in sustainable dieting. Dr. Meena designs plans that include your favourite foods in moderation — no starvation, no extreme restrictions.
Is the diet plan customized for me?
Yes — every plan is 100% personalized. We consider your medical history, taste preferences, budget, cooking habits and lifestyle before creating your plan.
How many times do I meet the doctor?
This depends on your plan — Basic includes 1 monthly session, Standard includes weekly sessions, and Premium offers unlimited consultations as needed.
Do you offer online consultations?
Yes! We offer video consultations via WhatsApp or Google Meet for patients outside Salem or those who prefer remote sessions. Contact us to schedule.
