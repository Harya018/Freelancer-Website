Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic
Tamil Cuisine

Founded in 1995 by the Annapoorna family, we have been serving the most authentic, home-style South Indian vegetarian food in Salem for over three decades. Every dish is prepared fresh each morning using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

From the humble Idli-Sambar breakfast to the grand afternoon Meals, our kitchen is a celebration of Tamil culinary heritage. We use only the freshest ingredients sourced locally every day.
