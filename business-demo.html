FAQ

Common Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with all types of small and medium businesses in Salem — restaurants, retail shops, clinics, salons, contractors, consultants, startups and more.
How long does GST registration take?
GST registration typically takes 7–10 working days from document submission. We handle the entire process — document preparation, portal filing, ARN tracking, and certificate delivery.
Do you offer one-time services or only monthly packages?
Both! We offer one-time services (GST registration, company registration, website creation) and monthly retainer packages for ongoing support. Contact us to discuss what suits your needs.
Can I meet you in person in Salem?
Absolutely! Our office is at Saradha College Road, Salem. Walk-ins are welcome during business hours (Mon–Sat 9AM–7PM), or call/WhatsApp to schedule an appointment.
What documents do I need to bring for the first consultation?
For the initial free consultation, no documents are needed — just come with your questions! If you're ready to proceed with a service, we'll provide you a clear document checklist based on your specific requirement.
