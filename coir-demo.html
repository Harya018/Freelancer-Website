Our Process

From Coconut to Your Door

🌴
Step 01
Raw Coir Harvested
Fresh coconut husk collected from local farms across Tamil Nadu. Only the finest fibres are selected.
🔨
Step 02
Cleaned & Processed
Husks are retted, dried and cleaned using traditional and modern methods to extract pure coir fibres.
🧵
Step 03
Handwoven by Artisans
Skilled Salem artisans weave, twist and craft each product with decades of traditional expertise.
📦
Step 04
Quality Checked & Shipped
Every product undergoes quality inspection before being carefully packed and shipped across India.
